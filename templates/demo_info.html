Enhanced Calculation Methodology

Core Formulas

Plant Capacity (kW) =
Monthly Consumption ÷ (Avg Irradiance × PR × 30)

Monthly Generation =
Capacity × Avg Irradiance × PR × 30

Investment (CAPEX) =
Capacity × Cost per kW

Annual Savings =
Yearly Generation × Tariff Rate

Payback Period =
Investment ÷ Annual Savings

CO₂ Saved =
Yearly Generation × 0.8 kg/kWh
